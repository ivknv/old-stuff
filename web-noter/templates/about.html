What is Noter?

Noter is an amazing site for keeping notes.

Who am I?

My name is Ivan Konovalov (Google+ profile).
I'm a programmer.
Programming — is my hobby and I really enjoy it.

Why?

Because there's no perfect tool for keeping notes and Noter is not an exception.
But Noter made differently, it has easier UI and it's open source (Repository on Github).

Noter vs. monsters like Evernote

I'm not trying to say that monsters like Evernote sucks.
I'm just trying to say that Noter is much lighter and it's definetly not a monster.
It's easy to use, fully free and simple.
And what about our monsters? Here's the list:

  1. Too big
  2. With limits of free usage
  3. Too Complicated

History

I've got an idea to create Noter after I got my phone (Huawei U8110) broken.
I used to always keep notes, that was a part of my common day. I used ColorNote for Android to do that.
But there was so many things... I had to write them somewhere.
Before it was my phone but what should I do when it's broken?
Of course I could write them on paper but that's just inconvenient.
So, after some months I decided to create Noter.
Because I wanted to create something my own, with my own suggestions, something that I really need right now.
